1. We start by getting to extensions and installing MySQL management tool.
2. In the explorer button, click on MySQL
    > In MySQL tab, the upper right corner shows a button, click on it
    > A field will come out asking for the name of the database host in 
    which you plan to connect.
        ex.: localhost
    > The next field will come out asking for the authentication. Type:
        ex.: root 
    > It will then ask for a password. For our example, we will not need
    one. Press enter.
    > It will the will automatically populated the next field with a port
    number. Press enter.
    > The last field as for SSL certificate, used for encription. This is 
    not necessary in our case. Press enter.
